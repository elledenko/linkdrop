# LinkDrop — Research Brief

## Market Overview

The content curation software market is valued at **$5.36B in 2025**, projected to reach **$17.6B by 2034** (13.7% CAGR). The creator/newsletter economy is exploding — Substack hit 5M paid subs, Beehiiv serves 350M monthly readers. People are paying $5-15/mo for tools in this space.

**Timing factor:** Pocket shut down in July 2025, displacing millions of users actively evaluating new tools.

## Competitors

| Product | What it does | Pricing | Gap |
|---------|-------------|---------|-----|
| Raindrop.io | Beautiful bookmark manager with collections | Free / $3.54/mo | No digest output, no sharing, no auto-summary |
| Readwise Reader | Read-later with highlights & AI summaries | $9.99/mo | Personal reading only, no sharing/publishing |
| elink.io | Save links → turn into newsletters/pages | Free / $15/mo | No auto-summarization, no daily cadence, dated UI |
| Curated.co | Newsletter curation platform | ~$25/mo | Built for pro newsletter operators, not casual daily use |
| Digest (usedigest.com) | Aggregates feeds into daily email | Free / $4.99/mo | Pulls from pre-configured sources, can't paste arbitrary links |
| Glasp | Social web highlighter | Free / $10/mo | Highlighting-focused, no digest output |
| Pinboard | Minimal fast bookmarking | $22-39/yr | No AI, no sharing, developer niche only |

## The Gap

No tool connects **"paste a link"** → **"auto-summarize"** → **"daily digest"** → **"share"** in one flow.

The market splits in two:
- **Save side** (Raindrop, Pinboard, Readwise) — great at organizing, zero publishing
- **Publish side** (Substack, Beehiiv, elink) — great at newsletters, require manual assembly

LinkDrop sits in the middle. The voice/personality layer — writing digests in your voice, ready to export per platform — is completely unserved.

## Target Users

1. Tech workers / developers — heavy link curators, many want link blogs but tooling is DIY
2. Content creators — share curated links as a content format on socials
3. VCs / investors — run curated newsletters for portfolio companies
4. Journalists / writers — professional info gatherers who share roundups
5. Knowledge workers — curate reading lists for teams, currently do it in Slack/Notion

## Go Signal

**Strong go.** Massive market with double-digit growth. Post-Pocket migration window open. No existing tool does paste → summarize → daily digest → share. Voice/personality angle is completely unserved. $5-10/mo price point validated.
